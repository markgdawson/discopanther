Way back in 2012, some cats stumbled upon each other and felt compelled to show the world what a good time should be: Sexy and damn right Groovy. Unfortunately, as individuals, they had no power to do so, and were overshadowed by other "Music" (such as pop, dubstep and metal). What could they do? Mightily morph into a single big cat....... Enter DISCO PANTHER

Panthersizer

Pantera Vellere

Believed by many to be forged in a night of passion between Duncan Jones and Jenna Jameson - Panthersizer inherited his fathers hard hitting skills and his mothers sexual appetite. Bullied as a child for his curly locks and pearly smooth skin Panthersizer fought off his enemies with powerful keyboard skills. Hardened on the schoolyard he can kill a full-grown man with a single chord from 30 paces.

Token Panther

Pantera Albus

Token panther was once the blackest panther of all. Till a freak accident in a bleaching factory turned him a sparkly white. Token Panther was born. He then spent the 80's campaigning for the erradication of dangerous chemicals, Princess Diana was a great admirer.

Sax Panther

Pantera Pumilio

He was raised by a remote tribe of funk-loving midgets in Fuji who were where the only race on earth to invented the saxophone before the wheel. Unfortunately he must have a internet connection to Fuji to operate bicycles.

Papa Panther

Pantera Rex

Lost at birth Papa panther was long thought to be dead. He was later found in Africa in 1994 raised in the wild by real Panthers in return teaching them to holler like James Brown. Still wild from his days in the Jungle his ability to stalk and pounce on unsuspecting prey is the thing of legend in wilder countries. A skill which he puts to good use in the urban jungle.

Hippie Panther

Pantera Animus

Abandoned at birth in a monastery in Tibet, Hippie developed unprecedented connection to all living things. Disillusioned by a celibate monks life he moved to the UK in the early 60s - where his radical new thinking freed the population and sparked a spiritual and musical revolution. He now lives in a remote shack on a diet of pure energy from mother earth and carrots surrounded by his following of select women whom he teaches about spiritual enlightenment.

Stick Panther

Pantera Auris Magnus

Not much is known about the origins of Stick. He was first recorded by early explorers in the heart of Africa teaching the native tribes how to lay down a juicy beat. He was then lost again until the 1963 where he and Hippie Panther lead the revolution. His power was weakened when a syndicate of evil corporations poisoned the minds of the masses with the drum machine. He's since been in hiding patiently waiting and plotting his revenge.

Grand Panther

Pantera Granditer

Known as the Godfather of Groove in many countries Grand panther was the youngest person to win the coveted Butlins international "Mr. Funk" award at the age of three. Some say he inspired William Shatners version of "Rocket Man", but in reality, for brief afternoon, they were the same being.

Stretch Panther

Pantera Procerus

Despite appearances Stretch panther was an ordinary teenager until a freak gamma radiation incident in his fathers science lab brought on uncontrollable growth. Despite the efforts of his father to reverse the effects no cure could be found. A glittering basketball career was brought to a halt when he and both cheerleading teams turned up late to a major international game. Stretch went on to hit the high notes as a singer.

Axe Panther

Pantera Absens

Once a rich and powerful businessman, Axe was driven underground when a secret experimental aftershave was lost from his company lab. Believed to be irresistibly powerful to the opposite sex and bestow the wearer with magical powers the mythical Panther-based scent is classified as a threat to national security in 27 countries. Rumoured to have a secret personal stash Axe is often seen sneaking off into the dark before a big gig.
